## 根本原因

## 成功标准

- [ ] NotebookLM 主路不变（queryNotebook 成功时行为不变）
- [ ] fallback 路径从 synthesis_archive 查最近 7 天注入历史上下文
- [ ] buildRuminationPrompt 非空 context 时注入历史反刍上下文区块
- [ ] NotebookLM 失败时 LLM 仍能看到历史反刍洞察
- [ ] CI 全部通过

## DoD

### 1. [PRESERVE] NotebookLM 主路 queryNotebook 调用保持不变
  Test: manual:node -e "const c=require('fs').readFileSync('packages/brain/src/rumination.js','utf8');if(!c.includes('queryNotebook(nbQuery,'))process.exit(1)"

### 2. [ARTIFACT] buildRuminationPrompt 非空 context 时注入历史反刍上下文区块
  Test: manual:node -e "const c=require('fs').readFileSync('packages/brain/src/rumination.js','utf8');if(!c.includes('历史反刍上下文'))process.exit(1)"

  Test: manual:node -e "const c=require('fs').readFileSync('packages/brain/src/rumination.js','utf8');if(!c.includes('synthesis_archive')||!c.includes('fallbackContext'))process.exit(1)"

  Test: manual:node -e "const c=require('fs').readFileSync('packages/brain/src/rumination.js','utf8');const lines=c.split('\n');const fbLine=lines.findIndex(l=>l.includes('Fallback'));const seg=lines.slice(fbLine,fbLine+12).join('\n');if(seg.includes(\"buildRuminationPrompt(learnings, memoryBlock, '')\"))process.exit(1)"
